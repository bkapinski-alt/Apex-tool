RULES THAT NEVER CHANGE
Risk per trade
0.3% always. Daily loss limit 0.6% — 2 losses and you stop for the day. Risk of ruin is 5 consecutive losing days.
Copy trading allowed
Apex explicitly permits copy trading across all your own PA accounts. All accounts must trade the same direction — no hedging.
Never move your stop
Your invalidation is clear at entry. Moving stops is how accounts breach drawdown.
Don't rush phases
Complete 2 full 6-payout cycles on 100K before stepping to 150K. Prove consistency first.
One eval at a time
Don't buy the next eval until the current one passes. Focus beats multitasking at this stage.
50% consistency per account
Tracked independently per PA — a big day on one account doesn't affect the others.
